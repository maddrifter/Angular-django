Email a Card

Upgrade Now!

The Email a Card extra is available on any ScrumDo premium plan. You can upgrade your plan here.

For information on the Email a Card extra, please see http://help.scrumdo.com/help/emailcard.html

Using Email a Card, you can send an email to create a new card in your workspace, without ever opening a web browser.

To get started, send an email to this special address created just for this workspace: {{emailctrl.extraConfig.address}}

Some guidelines to make sure the resulting card is exactly what you want:

  • The subject of the email will become the summary
  • The body of the email will become the detail field (optional)
In the body of the email, after the detail, you can optionally supply any of the following:
  • assignee: username, username2
  • due: 2015-01-01
  • tags: tag1, tag2
  • labels: label1, label2
  • points: 5
  • epic: 3
  • estimate: 3:00

You must send this email from an address associated with an account that has write access to the workspace.

Every card created in this manner will also get a tag of email-generated automatically applied.
