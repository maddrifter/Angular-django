Card Settings

These settings affect the fields you see on your cards.

Optional Fields

You can turn fields that you don't use off or experiment with new fields here.

Business Value Mode

Business value represents the value a given card is expected to generate or the business priority in which things are done.

Custom Fields

Custom fields are free-input text boxes that accompany each card. To use one, turn it on by selecting the checkbox, and give it a name in the text input.
